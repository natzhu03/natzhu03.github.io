Horse Stressors App

Redesigning Maryland Therapeutic Riding’s horse health data tracking system and operations.

Project Description:

Duration: May 2023 - Jul 2023

Team: Natalie Zhu, Amie Trang, Fatima Ortega, Clara Yoo, Eric Kim, Laolu Ganiy, Grace Guan, Aleksandr Molchagin, Angelo David

Role: Subteam Lead Designer

Previously, Maryland Therapeutic Riding (MTR) was inefficiently inputting data on their horse’s stress levels. On a team of designers at Develop For Good, I helped tackled this problem by designing a web application to streamline data input and tracking, and improve staff operations.

Project Achievements

> As one of two subteam Design leads, led the transformation of Maryland Therapeutic Riding’s horse health data tracking system and operations through the design of a new web-based SaaS product, increasing staff accessibility and operational efficiency.

> Directed essential stages of the design process to redesign client data and spreadsheets as a sophisticated, responsive website, including the storyboarding, wireframing, A/B testing, and low and high-fidelity prototyping phases.
