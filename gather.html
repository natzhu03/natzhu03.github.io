Gather - A Social Web App

A Digital Time Capsule for Reigniting Past Connections.

Project Description:

Duration: May 2023 - Jul 2023

Team: Natalie Zhu, Tsung-Han Yu, Jaein Kim

*Note: All designs shown of "Gather" are by Jaein Kim

At the 2023 Brown hackathon, my team was prompted to develop a project aligned with the Hackathon theme "Campfire." Drawing inspiration from the warm sentiment of gathering and reunion associated with a campfire, we developed a social web application designed to reconnect old friends through a user-friendly registration and messaging system.

Project Demo

My team developed a social web application, which we named 'Gather', which enabled users to register to be sent future customized text reminders to reunite with specific past friends. I took on our team role of front-end developer, and later conducted application unit testing. My team achieved the following:

> Integrated Twilio into the application to enable scheduled text messages to users as reminders to reconnect with certain friend(s).

> Constructed a Flask application testing framework using pytest, validating the appâ€™s functionality and ensuring a smooth user experience.

> Won Best First Hack Award at 2023 Hack@Brown hackathon.
